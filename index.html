The artist behind the brush

Meet Anna

An Italian artist now calling Austin home, Anna discovered her passion for face and body painting in 2014 with a simple 10-color kit. Within a year she was competing internationally — and hasn't stopped since.

She's placed at the World Bodypainting Championship in Austria, competed in South Korea, and taken 1st place at the Texas Body Paint Festival. Her client roster includes Google, EA Games, Warner Bros, the Westin, and the Hilton.

But what drives Anna isn't trophies — it's the moment a child sees themselves transformed into a superhero, or a brand sees their vision come alive on skin. Every brushstroke tells a story.
